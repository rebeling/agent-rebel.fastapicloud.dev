- **AI gateway:** A policy and routing boundary for AI traffic.
- **Aggregator:** A service exposing many providers through one commercial account.
- **BYOK:** Bring your own provider key.
- **Control plane:** Configuration, administration, and governance layer.
- **Data plane:** Live request-processing path.
- **Denial of wallet:** Cost exhaustion caused by abusive or runaway requests.
- **Fallback:** A secondary route used after defined failure conditions.
- **Guardrail:** A policy or model that checks, transforms, blocks, or classifies content.
- **Provider:** A company or infrastructure service serving models.
- **Semantic cache:** A cache that reuses responses for similar rather than identical prompts.
- **Time to first token:** Delay before the first streamed token arrives.
- **Virtual key:** A gateway-issued credential associated with identity and policy.
- **Virtual model:** A stable gateway model name mapped to one or more physical backends.
- **ZDR:** Zero data retention.
